Heavily inspired by 90's Japanese Horror, "Hermit's Sacred Band" is a tale of rock n' roll and terror, with a 1970's backdrop.

Zombies! Aliens! Rock N' Roll! Love!

Concept

Survival Horror
- Make your way home thru the terror-filled nights of Auto City. Contend with bizarre creatures and mind controlled humans!
Social Sim
- You must get to know the movers and shakers in the scene in order to get clues to where the next show is, all while keeping tabs on a simulated calendar system!
Role-Playing Game
- Meet a colorful cast of characters, gain experience points and mold your character as you aim for survival and rock n' roll glory!
